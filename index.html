IBM App Connect allows you to simply and securely connect applications and data across all of your environments by supporting a wide range of integration styles. These range from traditional SOA to modern event, API, and microservices-based integration styles. IBM App Connect provides a single integration tool for your entire business. Whether you are a line of Business (LOB) professional in a small to medium business (SMB) looking to quickly build workflows that automate tasks, or a seasoned integrator in a large enterprise who wants to integrate and expose data across a complex ecosystem, IBM App Connect provides powerful, user-centric tooling that enables you to achieve a range of use cases.

IBM App Connect helps businesses to:

  • Connect their applications and systems by supporting multiple integration styles and helping to achieve most integration use cases
  • Make integration possible for all users (technical and non-technical) to help automate tasks and become more productive
  • Move to a hybrid environment by enabling them to connect across on-premises and cloud systems and applications
